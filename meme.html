Meme Mashup


This meme mashup was made on Photoshop and is a combination of multiple memes to tell one story. In the mashup, there are multiple silly cats fighting each other in Fortnite. Because Fortnite is still massive, I thought it would be the best place to put these cats in. Each cat has a purpose, whether that is to actively fight and react or just be dead/a bystander. I also included the two Soyjaks pointing at the scene because I think it pushes the viewer to have that same reaction.
